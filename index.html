ABOUT US

I'm Chris Hansen, and Hansen Home Inspections, PLLC is my company.
When you set an appointment with Hansen Home Inspections, I'm the one who inspects your home...a Professional Inspector licensed by the Texas Real Estate Commission (#23601).
I typically schedule one inspection a day so that I can spend as much time as necessary with my clients and the house to insure a quality inspection, and I issue a written, full color report with pictures the same day. I never try to upsell my clients for services or items that they don't want or need, and I don't take money for selling third-party services.
If you want a thorough Home Inspection for a fair price, contact me today!

SERVICES

What's included in your inspection?

Structural

Foundation, Grading and Drainage, Roof Structure; Attic & Crawlspace (where accessible), Walls, Ceilings & Floors, Doors, Windows, Stairways, Fireplace and Chimney, Porches, Balconies, Decks & Carports.

Electrical Sytem

Service Entrance, Panels, Branch Circuits, Connected Devices & Fixtures.

Heating & Cooling

Heating & Cooling Equipment(weather permitting for some AC systems), Duct System, Chases & Vents.

Plumbing Systems

Plumbing Supply, Distribution Systems & Fixtures, Drains, Wastes, and Vents, Water Heating Equipment, Hydro-Massage Therapy Equipment.

Appliances

Dishwasher(s), Garbage Disposal, Range Hood & Exhaust, Stove, Oven & Cooktop, Microwave, Bathroom Vents & Heaters, Garage Door Opener, Dryer Venting.

Optional Systems

Sprinklers, Outbuildings, Pools, Hot Tubs, Spas, Wells, and Septic Systems.

Types of Structures

Single Family Homes; Multi-Family Homes (up to 4 units); Condominiums, Apartment Rentals.
